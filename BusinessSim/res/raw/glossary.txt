1. APR(interest) - A rate that is charged on a loan
                                based on the amount burrowed.

2. Bankrupt - A business that is unable to pay their debt.

3. Business - The practice engaging in commerce.

4. Debt - Something that is owed or due.

5. Demand - The quantity of a producted based on the 
                       price the buyer is willing to pay.

6. Loan - Borrowing money temporarily at the cost of 
                 interest.

7. Price - The value of a product.

8. Profit - Money that is made in a business.

9. Sales - An exchange of a product typically with 
                  money.

10. Supply - The quantity of a product that the seller is 
                      willing to have based on the price they are 
                      selling it for.
